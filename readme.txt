This game is made for two (human) players. 

RULES:
1) Each player starts with 12 pieces and a random amount of money.  Take as many of your opponent's pieces as you can in the alloted amount of turns
2) You may only jump a maximum of one space (move being different from a jump)
3) Jump = Taking an opponent's piece, Move = Moving your piece forward to an empty diagonal space nearby
3) Once you reach the end of the opponent's side, your piece no longer move

e is the Player One's pieces
o is the player Two pieces
Please choose a board location with first char of A-H and second char of 1-8
